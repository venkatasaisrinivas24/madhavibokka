Who We Are

Madhavi Narendher Reddy Community Outreach is a community-focused initiative working specifically for the welfare of Moinabad, Telangana.

We bridge the gap between citizens and solutions, working tirelessly on public grievance support and local development activities. Our vision is a self-sustained, hygienic, and thriving community where every individual has access to basic welfare and support.

Our Initiatives

Social Welfare

Providing essential support to the underprivileged. We organize drives for health awareness, distribute basic necessities, and ensure government schemes reach the beneficiaries.

Grievance Support

A dedicated channel for the public to voice their concerns. We assist in resolving civic issues such as water supply, electricity, and sanitation by coordinating with local authorities.

Local Development

Fostering the growth of Moinabad through infrastructure monitoring, cleanliness drives (Swachh Bharat initiatives), and promoting sustainable village development.
